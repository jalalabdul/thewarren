About Us

The Warren was born out of passion for great food. It is the creation of chef Deri Reed and Vegetable Grower Steffan Lemke Elms who are both thriving to create Wales’s most sustainable eatery.

Deri, also known as The Ethical Chef has had a lifelong dream to set up his own eatery and it was when meeting Steffan that gave him the confidence that it could be done.

Steffan, also know as The Welly Man (more on this later) has been growing vegetables organically for the last 10 years and wants to show the world how it’s done.

Together they are setting up “Project Grow,” a community project whereby they plan to be demonstrating through workshops in local schools and communities how to get more green fingered through cooking, growing and healthy eating! projectgrowCIC
